Overview

High-stakes decisions need
a focused place to land.

When Verint's Agentic AI closes a $600K life insurance policy, no automated system should carry that alone. Supervisor Hub is where the AI hands off to a human - with full context, a 3-minute clock, and AI-drafted rationale already prepared.

⚡
Real-time interrupt
A forced-acknowledgment modal - not a toast, not a badge - that stops the supervisor and demands a decision. Because $600K deserves interruption.
🤖
AI-drafted rationale
When the supervisor decides, Copilot has already prepared the words that explain why. They review, edit, and submit - never starting from a blank page.
📋
Full audit trail
Every decision is logged with its rationale, timestamp, and decision maker. Compliance isn't an afterthought - it's built into the moment of action.
