The User

The person holding the line
when the AI can't.

A contact center supervisor runs 8-15 agents at once. They're coaching mid-call, watching queue health, chasing metrics, handling escalations - all simultaneously, every shift.

And somewhere in that chaos, a $600K life insurance application is waiting for their approval. A real person is on hold. The AI got as far as it could. Now it's on them.

Before Supervisor Hub, that moment had nowhere to land properly.

The Flow

Three minutes.
Three possible outcomes.
Zero guesswork.

The supervisor enters the moment the AI can't proceed alone. What happens next is a precise sequence - designed so that every decision is fast, informed, and defensible.

1
Trigger
The AI gets as far as it can
Verint's Agentic AI handles the interaction autonomously - until it hits a threshold it can't cross alone. A $500K+ life insurance policy, a compliance flag, a legal edge case. The AI creates an authorization request and stops.
Policy: $600,000 · Risk score: 68/100
2
Interrupt
The supervisor can't miss it
A full-screen modal takes over. Customer name, policy amount, risk score, and a live SLA countdown. It can't be dismissed without acknowledgment - because a $600K decision shouldn't be silently swiped away.
⏱ Clock starts: 3 minutes
3
Review
Everything needed to decide - assembled and waiting
The case view brings together policy details, all compliance checks, AI analysis, and comparable case statistics. No system-switching. No searching. The supervisor reads, evaluates, and decides.
4
Decision
Three paths. All documented.
Whatever the outcome, the Copilot has drafted the rationale. The supervisor reviews, edits, and submits. Nothing leaves without a paper trail.
