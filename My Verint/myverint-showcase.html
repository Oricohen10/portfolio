The Challenge

The workforce had no
unified mobile experience.

Before MyVerint, every role worked with a different set of tools. None of them mobile-first. None of them built with the frontline employee in mind. The goal was never just consolidation. It was giving each role something that actually fit how they work.

👤
Frontline Agent
~7,000 people
Checking the schedule meant a desktop login before every shift
Time-off requests meant a multi-step HR form, on a computer only
No way to check leave balance or shift details on the go
Daily tools simply did not exist on mobile
👥
Team Manager
~800 people
Reviewing team performance meant opening a dedicated desktop app
Approving time-off requests while away from the desk created delays for the whole team
No single view of availability, pending requests, and performance in one place
Oversight was tied to a fixed workstation
📊
WFO Analyst
~200 people
Real-time KPI monitoring lived on a desktop dashboard and nowhere else
No mobile alerts when staffing dropped below threshold during critical hours
AI insights and forecasting data had no mobile delivery layer
Real-time data was stuck at the desk
The App

Five tabs.
Every workflow.

MyVerint organizes every workflow into five purpose-built tabs. Agents, managers, and back-office teams each get a view designed around their role, with an AI assistant that ties it all together.

Conversational AI
Ask AI
The conversational entry point for every task. Employees ask in natural language and get answers backed by live WFO data, without ever leaving the chat.
  • Natural language input
    Ask anything the way you would ask a colleague
  • Live WFO data
    Leave balances, schedules, and KPIs surfaced inline
  • One-tap actions
    Submit a request, approve, or confirm in a single tap
  • Full conversation history
    Every action logged with context and timestamp
Time & Shifts
Schedule
A full calendar view of upcoming shifts, color-coded by type. Employees can read their week at a glance and take action without switching apps.
  • Month and week views
    Switch between views to match how far ahead you plan
  • Shift type color coding
    Read your week at a glance without counting rows
  • Shift swap requests
    Initiate a change directly from any shift in the calendar
  • Activity feed
    Schedule changes and approvals surface in real time
Personal KPIs
Performance
Personal KPI dashboard with AI-powered coaching insights. Agents see where they stand and what to fix, without a manager walking them through it.
  • Personal KPI cards
    Key metrics with trend indicators, no manager needed
  • AI coaching insights
    Specific, actionable suggestions based on your data
  • Team benchmark comparison
    See where you stand relative to your peers
  • Historical trends
    Week-over-week and month-over-month performance views
Leave & Approvals
Requests
All time-off, shift change, and schedule requests in one place. No more separate portals, no more calling HR for a status update.
  • All request types in one place
    Time off, shift swap, and flexible start: one flow for all
  • Real-time status tracking
    Pending, approved, or rejected: always visible
  • Automatic routing
    Requests reach the right approver without any manual steps
  • Push notifications
    Status changes surface immediately on the employee’s phone

Ask AI

The feature that
changed everything.

Ask AI started as a convenient shortcut. It became the primary way people use the app. Instead of navigating menus or calling HR, employees just ask. The AI reads intent, pulls live data, and completes the action in a single conversation.
